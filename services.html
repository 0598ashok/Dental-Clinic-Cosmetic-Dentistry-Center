World-Class Dental Care

Cosmetic & Restorative Services

From premium porcelain veneers and advanced dental implants to clear aligners and preventive hygiene, our Chicago specialists combine clinical precision with cosmetic artistry.

01 / COSMETIC ARTISTRY

Cosmetic Smile Design & Aesthetic Makeovers

Vance Cosmetic Dentistry is Chicago's premier studio for smile transformations. We specialize in custom-crafted porcelain veneers, tooth bonding, and aesthetic restorations designed to match your natural facial symmetry.

Using state-of-the-art digital dental mapping and Virtual Reality smile mockups, we construct customized treatment paths that deliver flawless, long-lasting cosmetic results.

Cosmetic Options Include:

  • ✓ Custom Porcelain Veneers & Lumineers
  • ✓ Professional Zoom! Laser Teeth Whitening
  • ✓ Aesthetic Composite Bonding & Re-contouring
  • ✓ Digital Smile Design VR Simulations
  • ✓ Metal-Free Porcelain Crowns & Inlays

Restorative Services:

  • ✓ Single-Tooth & Full-Arch Dental Implants
  • ✓ Bio-compatible Dental Crowns & Bridges
  • ✓ Pain-Free Root Canal Therapy & Endodontics
  • ✓ Advanced Periodontal (Gum Disease) Care
  • ✓ Precision Partial & Full Dentures
02 / STRUCTURAL HEALTH

Restorative Care & Advanced Dental Implants

Reclaim your smile's original structure, strength, and function. Whether you are dealing with damaged, decaying, or missing teeth, our dental specialists provide high-precision reconstructive procedures.

From single dental implants to full-mouth restorations, we use bio-compatible materials to ensure long-term health and stability.

03 / ORTHODONTICS & PREVENTION

Invisible Aligners & Comprehensive Preventive Wellness

A healthy smile starts with proper alignment and dedicated prevention. We offer modern clear aligner solutions like Invisalign to straighten your teeth discreetly and comfortably.

Combined with comprehensive routine cleanings, advanced intraoral scans, and early decay detection, we keep your natural enamel strong and your gums perfectly healthy.

Preventive & Aligner Care:

  • ✓ Invisalign® Clear Aligner Therapy
  • ✓ Advanced Oral Hygiene & Deep Cleanings
  • ✓ 3D Digital Intraoral Scanning & Diagnostics
  • ✓ Professional Guard Therapy for Bruxism/TMJ
  • ✓ Preventive Fluoride & Sealant Applications
