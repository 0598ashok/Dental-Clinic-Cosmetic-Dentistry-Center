Common Clinic Inquiries

Review the answers below to find immediate clarification on consultation processes, scheduling details, and practice protocols.

Do you accept dental insurance protocols? +

Yes, we accept major dental PPO insurance plans. Our administrative team will handle all coordination claims and pre-authorization approvals to maximize your procedural coverage details.

How long does a Smile Makeover consultation take? +

An initial smile design consultation takes approximately 60 minutes. This includes 3D mapping scans, intraoral photographic logs, and personal alignment planning with our specialist team.

Are custom veneer mockups available before surgery? +

Absolutely. We construct virtual 3D mockups and trial shells that fit over your teeth. This allows you to evaluate shape, length, and shade aesthetics prior to initiating any permanent adjustments.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We require at least 24 hours notice for calendar slot rescheduling. This allows us to reallocate advanced surgical equipment and staff resources to other waiting list patient schedules.
