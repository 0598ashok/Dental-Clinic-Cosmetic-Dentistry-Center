Who We Are

The Art & Science of Smiling

At Vance Cosmetic Dentistry, we believe a smile transformation requires both clinical excellence and detailed visual artistry.

Our Mission

Advanced Facial Aesthetics & Medical Quality

Most dental practices focus on quick fixes. We look at the larger picture. We evaluate your unique jaw proportions, smile curvature, lip support, and tooth structure to design a custom dental makeover plan that matches your facial characteristics.

By combining computer-guided 3D scans and premium hand-finished dental materials, we provide results that feel completely natural and last a lifetime.

Three Technology Pillars

  • ✓ Digital Scan Precision: We use state-of-the-art intraoral digital scanner mapping to model teeth structures without messy dental molds.
  • ✓ Masterful Ceramics: Our veneers and crowns are fabricated from pure bio-compatible lithium disilicate crystal for outstanding strength and life-like translucency.
  • ✓ Interactive Patient Care: View procedure reports, treatment logs, and direct appointments messaging via our Patient Portal SaaS.

Vance Chronicles

Our Timeline Journey

From a single dental chair to virtual reality cosmetic mapping. Track our key milestones in clinical and digital aesthetic precision.

2008

Clinic Founded

Dr. Evelyn Vance establishes the cosmetic dental center in Chicago with a focus on advanced custom veneers.

2013

Goes Digital

Integrates high-speed iTero digital scanners, eliminating chemical plaster molds completely for absolute clinical accuracy.

2018

AACD Gold Standard

Granted gold cosmetic excellence recognition by the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry for restorative art.

2022

Introduces online treatment mapping, live chat integration, and automated progress logging via our digital store architecture.

2026

Smile VR Previews

Launches virtual reality diagnostic previews, letting patients walk through their new smile makeover before ceramic fabrication.
