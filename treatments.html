The Vance Protocol

Your Smile Design Pathway

Understanding our detailed clinical and digital design stages helps you confidently co-design your custom smile transformation.

01

Comprehensive Digital Mapping

We start with high-speed digital scanners that capture thousands of frames/sec to construct an interactive 3D teeth blueprint. There are no messy, plaster chemical molds, ensuring immediate comfort.

02

Aesthetic Architecture Design

Dr. Vance reviews bone proportions and lip curvature mapping. We adjust teeth length, curvature, and alignment digitally to render a mock preview. You can approve or request changes before we mill.

03

Computer-Guided Diamond Milling

Once approved, the restoration models are sent to our CAD/CAM diamond milling machine. The machine carves veneers or crowns from pure crystalline disilicate blocks in less than 40 minutes for flawless fit.

04

Micro-Layering & Bonding

Our ceramist hand-glazes veneer edges to mimic realistic enamel. We then use medical-grade adhesive composites and light curing to bond restorations to teeth structures for long-term durability.
